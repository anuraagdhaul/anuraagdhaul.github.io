Largo (Symphony #9)

Largo from Symphony #9, commonly known as "In the New World," is one of Antonin Dvorak's most famous pieces. The movement is characterized by its emotional, expressive, and slow tempo. For many people, this piece is one of the most memorable passages in the entire symphony, capturing the soul of both the New World (America) and Dvořák himself. As this piece is meant for a duet, I played (the melody) alongside my clarinet instructor (who played the harmony).
