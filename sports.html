Soccer

At the end of each sports season at ASH, selected teams participate in the International Schools Sport Tournament. In this tournament, the eight particpating teams (including ASH) are split up into two groups, playing each other to qualify for the semi-finals and eventually the finals. I have participated in this tournament twice: once in Paris, France, and recently in Vienna, Austria.
